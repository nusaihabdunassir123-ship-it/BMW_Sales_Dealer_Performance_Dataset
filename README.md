# BMW Sales Dealer Performance Analysis 🚗📊
📌 Project Overview

The BMW Sales Dealer Performance Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Tableau project designed to analyze dealership sales performance across different regions, vehicle segments, and BMW models.

This dashboard provides business stakeholders with actionable insights into revenue generation, profitability, sales trends, and top-performing products, enabling data-driven decision-making.

🎯 Project Objectives
Monitor overall dealership sales performance.
Analyze revenue and profit trends.
Identify top-performing BMW models.
Compare sales performance across regions.
Evaluate contribution by vehicle segment.
Enable interactive analysis through filters and dashboards.

📈 Key Insights
Certain regions consistently generate higher revenue than others.
SUV models contribute the largest share of sales revenue.
A small number of top-performing models drive a significant portion of overall revenue.
Monthly sales trends reveal peak sales periods and seasonal patterns.
Profitability varies across regions and vehicle segments.
🚀 Business Value

This dashboard helps dealership managers and business analysts:

Track sales performance in real time.
Identify high-performing regions and products.
Improve strategic planning and forecasting.
Support data-driven business decisions.
Quickly explore insights through interactive filtering.
